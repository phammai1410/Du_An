# 3. COURSE DESCRIPTIONS

The graduation thesis is an independent research project conducted by students under the guidance of instructors, helping to evaluate the knowledge and abilities to which students have accumulated during their study period and practical applicability as well.

The Graduation thesis module requires students to intern at a specific organization/business for at least 10 weeks.

The student's task is to research, learn, practice, evaluate the current state of professional business/organization and propose solutions and recommendations to solve problems/limitations from reality. on the basis of equipped and trained professional knowledge. At the end of internship, students are required to complete a academic research report and defend in front of a evaluation panel.

# 9. COURSE REQUIREMENT & EXPECTATION

## 9.1 Regulations on Attendance and Participation

Students are responsible for attending all meetings with instructors and working supervisors according to the requirements of the internship unit. In case of not meeting/working with the instructor due to reasonable reasons, there must be complete and suitable proof documents.

- Students who miss more than 3 class sessions, whether with or without reason, will be considered to have failed to complete the course and must re-register.

## 9.1 Regulations on Submission:

- The course is conducted on the principle of respect for learners and teachers. Any behavior that affects the teaching and learning process is strictly prohibited.

- Students must submit the results of running turnitin to check the overlap of the graduation thesis, confirmed by the instructor, attached immediately after the Declaration.

- Students interning at the unit need to have a certificate of internship and comments from the internship facility, attached immediately after the Conclusion of the graduation internship topic (Dissertation).

- Students are responsible for saving the detailed outline and manuscript with the instructor's autograph to serve inspection work.

- Students who do not submit the report after 5 days on time will be considered as not submitting the assignment. For each day of late submission, 1 point will be deducted. Students who do not approve the preliminary outline or detailed outline are not entitled to submit a final report.
