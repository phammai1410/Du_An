# 3. COURSE DESCRIPTIONS

The "Digital Transformation" course aims to provide students with in-depth business insights based on current and future technology trends, and at the same time raise learners' awareness of the rapid development of the industry. technology, mindset and ability to adapt to that change. Digital transformation is a comprehensive transformation process in an organization/enterprise, from leadership thinking to production and business processes, organizational structure, corporate culture, etc., and finally the transformation of digital transformation. technology side. The knowledge sharing module on digital transformation helps learners to apply the digital transformation process fluently to organizations/enterprises as well as advise leaders on how to organize, deploy, choose solutions and technology. digital transformation technology. The last part of the course talks about the area that is currently undergoing the most powerful digital transformation, which is the banking sector.
